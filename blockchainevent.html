Blockchain Event Visuals

Promotional and event branding visuals designed for a blockchain & Web3 audience.

Role: Graphic Designer
Type: Event Branding, Posters
Year: 2025

Project Overview

This project involved designing promotional visuals for a blockchain-focused event. The challenge was to balance technical credibility with bold, eye-catching design that would stand out across social media and event promotions.

Visual Concept

The visual direction leaned toward dark backgrounds, neon accents, and futuristic typography. Network-inspired graphics and strong contrast were used to reflect the decentralized and cutting-edge nature of blockchain.

Visual concept and style exploration

Event Posters & Creatives

A flexible poster system was created to support multiple speakers, schedules, and platforms while maintaining a cohesive look across all materials.

Outcome & Learnings

This project sharpened my ability to design for highly competitive feeds, experiment with expressive typography, and create visuals that communicate complex ideas quickly. It reinforced the importance of hierarchy and contrast in fast-scroll environments.
