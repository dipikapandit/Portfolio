Hotel Brand Visuals

Visual identity and Instagram creatives designed for a premium hospitality brand.

Role: Graphic & UI Designer
Type: Branding, Social Media
Year: 2025

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create a cohesive and premium visual identity for a hotel brand, primarily focused on Instagram content. The designs needed to communicate luxury, warmth, and trust while maintaining strong visual hierarchy for mobile audiences.

Visual Direction

The visual system uses warm tones, elegant typography, and minimal layouts. Emphasis was placed on spacing, contrast, and consistency across all creatives.

Moodboard and visual direction

Instagram Creatives

A flexible content system was designed to support promotions, announcements, and brand storytelling while keeping the feed visually consistent.

Outcome & Learnings

This project strengthened my understanding of text hierarchy, brand consistency, and designing for real-world social media constraints. The final visuals feel premium while remaining practical for daily content updates.
